Stories Walls Tell

Documentation of graffiti within Liman district of Novi Sad

This is a project that started as a way to kill time. In the process of documenting graffiti, I realized that they have historical, artistic and cultural significance.
Each piece of graffiti implies a writer; a real person that existed at a point in time, who chose to leave his mark on the world in some small way.
In the future, historians will likely examine graffiti when trying to piece together the fragments of a dead culture, much like how we percieve the ancient cultures today.
While buildings will crumble and graffiti will fade over time, this archive will not, and it represents man's effort to preserve our cultural heritage for future generations.
